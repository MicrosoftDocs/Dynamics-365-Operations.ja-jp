### <a name="best-price-only-within-priority-always-compound-across-priority"></a><span data-ttu-id="3480e-141">優先順位内のみの最良価格、常に優先順位間で組み入れ</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3480e-141">Best price only within priority, always compound across priority</span></span>
