[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="eede6-104">減価償却方法は、固定資産の取得年および処分年の両方で減価償却を計算するタイミングと方法を決定するのに使用されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eede6-104">Depreciation conventions are used to determine when and how depreciation is calculated for both the year when the fixed asset is acquired and the year when the fixed asset is disposed of.</span></span>
