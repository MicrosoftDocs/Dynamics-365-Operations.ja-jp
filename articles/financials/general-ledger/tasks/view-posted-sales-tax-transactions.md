# <a name="view-posted-sales-tax-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-103">転記された消費税トランザクションの表示</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-103">View posted sales tax transactions</span></span>

[!include[task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-104">必要な内容に応じて、さまざまなページから転記済売上税トランザクションを表示できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-104">You can view posted sales tax transactions from various page depending on what you need to view.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-105">このタスクでは、USMF というデモ会社を使用します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-105">This task uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-106">[税] > [照会およびレポート] > [売上税の照会] > [転記済売上税] へ移動します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-106">Go to Tax > Inquiries and reports > Sales tax inquiries > Posted sales tax.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-107">[フィルターの表示] をクリックします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-107">Click Show filters.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-108">ページを閉じます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-108">Close the page.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-109">[税] > [間接税] > [売上税] > [売上税決済期間] の順に移動します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-109">Go to Tax > Indirect taxes > Sales tax > Sales tax settlement periods.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-110">[間隔] セクションを展開します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-110">Expand the Period intervals section.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-111">必要な間隔を選択します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-111">Select the interval you are interested in.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-112">また、選択した決済期間間隔における転記済売上税トランザクションを表示できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-112">Display posted sales tax transactions for the selected settlement period interval.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-113">さらに、転記済売上税トランザクションの一覧をフィルター処理できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-113">You can further filter the list of posted sales tax transactions.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-114">ページを閉じます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-114">Close the page.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-115">[税] > [間接税] > [売上税] > [売上税コード] の順に移動します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-115">Go to Tax > Indirect taxes > Sales tax > Sales tax codes.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-116">[アクション] ペインで [売上税コード] をクリックします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-116">On the Action Pane, click Sales tax code.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="5c0e4-117">[転記済売上税] をクリックします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c0e4-117">Click Posted sales tax.</span></span>
