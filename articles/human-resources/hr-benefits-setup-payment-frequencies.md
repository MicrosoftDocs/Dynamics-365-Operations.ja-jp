<span data-ttu-id="2d53c-104">Microsoft Dynamics 365 Human Resources は、支払頻度を使用して年間給付金給与を計算し、従業員が各支払期間に支払う給付金割増金額とプロバイダーに支払われる頻度を決定します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d53c-104">Microsoft Dynamics 365 Human Resources uses payment frequencies to calculate the annual benefit salary, determine the benefit premium amount an employee pays each pay period, and how often providers are paid.</span></span>
