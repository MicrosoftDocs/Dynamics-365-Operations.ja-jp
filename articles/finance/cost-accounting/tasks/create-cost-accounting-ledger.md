[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-104">原価会計元帳は、レポート単位の全体を示しています。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-104">A cost accounting ledger represents the overall reporting unit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-105">原価要素の分析コード、統計分析コード、会計カレンダー、および通貨によって定義されています。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-105">It is defined by a cost element dimension, statistical dimension, fiscal calendar, and currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-106">法人の概念として、認識不可能です。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-106">It is agnostic to the concept of legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-107">法人とそのデータは、多くの原価会計元帳と関連付けることができます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-107">A legal entity and its data can be associated with many cost accounting ledgers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-108">この記録では、USP2 デモ データ会社を使用します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-108">This recording uses the USP2 demo data company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-109">[原価計算] > [元帳の設定] > [原価会計元帳] へ、移動します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-109">Go to Cost accounting > Ledger setup > Cost accounting ledgers.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="b0b6a-117">[保存] をクリックします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b0b6a-117">Click Save.</span></span>
