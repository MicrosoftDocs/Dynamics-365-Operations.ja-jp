## <a name="loan-item-to-a-worker"></a><span data-ttu-id="76076-107">作業者への貸与品目</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76076-107">Loan item to a worker</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="76076-108">[人事管理] > [作業者] > [貸与品目] > [貸与された設備] の順に移動します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76076-108">Go to Human resources > Workers > Loan items > Loaned equipment.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="76076-110">[個人] フィールドで、値を入力または選択します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76076-110">In the Person field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="76076-113">[保存] をクリックします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76076-113">Click Save.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="76076-114">ページを閉じます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76076-114">Close the page.</span></span>

## <a name="return-a-loan-item"></a><span data-ttu-id="76076-115">貸与品目の返品</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76076-115">Return a loan item</span></span>
