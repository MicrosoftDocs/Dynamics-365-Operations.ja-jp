### <a name="purchase-requisition-expenditure-reviewers"></a><span data-ttu-id="1a752-191">購買要求支出確認者</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1a752-191">Purchase requisition expenditure reviewers</span></span>
