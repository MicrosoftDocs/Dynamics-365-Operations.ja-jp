# <a name="landed-cost-module"></a><span data-ttu-id="266c1-103">陸揚原価モジュール</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-103">Landed cost module</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-104">**陸揚原価** モジュールは、メーカーから倉庫にインポートされた船荷に対する完全な財務および物流管理をユーザーに提供することによって、企業がインバウンド出荷作業を合理化するのに役立ちます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-104">The **Landed cost** module helps businesses streamline inbound shipping operations by giving users complete financial and logistical control over imported freight, from the manufacturer to the warehouse.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-105">輸入品の場合、陸揚原価が各輸入品目の合計原価の 40% 以上を占める可能性があります。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-105">For imported goods, landed costs can account for 40 percent or more of the total cost of each imported item.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-106">そのため、陸揚原価の正確な見積を提供するのは課題となります。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-106">Therefore, the challenge is to provide accurate estimates for landed costs.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-107">企業では、陸揚原価を使用して次のタスクを完了できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-107">Businesses can use Landed cost to complete the following tasks:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="266c1-108">航海作成時の陸揚原価を見積もります。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-108">Estimate landed costs at the time of voyage creation.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="266c1-109">1 回の航海で、陸揚原価を複数の品目と発注書または移動オーダーに分割します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-109">Apportion landed costs to multiple items and purchase orders or transfer orders in a single voyage.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="266c1-110">陸揚原価を認識することにより、物理的な場所間の商品の移動をサポートします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-110">Support the transfer of goods between physical locations by recognizing landed costs.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="266c1-111">輸送中の商品の見越計上を認識します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-111">Recognize accruals for goods in transit.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-112">陸揚原価は、間接陸揚原価に対して正確かつ適切なタイミングで原価見積を提供します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-112">Landed cost provides accurate and timely cost estimates for overhead landed costs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-113">同時に、拡張されたサプライ チェーンへの財務および物流の可視性が向上します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-113">At the same time, it provides increased financial and logistical visibility into the extended supply chain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-114">また、原価計算および原価計算エラーの管理を減らすのにも役立ちます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-114">It also helps reduce the administration of costing and costing errors.</span></span>

## <a name="highlights"></a><span data-ttu-id="266c1-115">特徴</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-115">Highlights</span></span>

### <a name="voyages"></a><span data-ttu-id="266c1-116">航海</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-116">Voyages</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-117">陸揚原価では、航海とは、出庫場所から指定された期間に特定の一連の目的地を経由して、指定された入庫倉庫ロケーションへの別個の移動を示します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-117">In Landed cost, a voyage is a distinct movement from an outbound location, through a specific set of destinations over a specified period, to a specified inbound warehouse location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-118">航海の 1 つのコンテナーまたは複数のコンテナーのいずれかに発注書および注文明細行が追加でき、原価は品目明細行に正しく割り当てられます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-118">Purchase orders and order lines can be added to either a single container or multiple containers for a voyage, and the costs will be correctly allocated back to the item line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-119">注文および注文明細行は、1 回の航海で法人全体に追加することもできます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-119">Orders and order lines can also be added across legal entities for a single voyage.</span></span>

### <a name="item-ownership"></a><span data-ttu-id="266c1-120">品目の所有権</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-120">Item ownership</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-121">Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management では、通常、商品は倉庫目的地で入庫されてから、請求されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-121">In Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management, goods are typically received at the warehouse destination and then invoiced.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-122">ただし、ほとんどの国際貿易協定では、企業は商品が出港地を離れた時点から、物理的に入庫される前に所有権を取得します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-122">However, under most international trade agreements, a business takes ownership of goods from the time when they leave the original port, before they have been physically received.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-123">陸揚原価により、企業は商品が物理的に入庫される前に請求を行うことができます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-123">Landed cost enables businesses to invoice goods before they have been physically received.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-124">この概念は、輸送中の商品注文と呼ばれます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-124">This concept is known as a goods in transit order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-125">元の発注書が請求された後に、商品の入庫を管理するための新しい注文タイプが作成されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-125">It creates a new type of order to manage the receipt of goods after the original purchase order has been invoiced.</span></span>

### <a name="costs"></a><span data-ttu-id="266c1-126">原価</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-126">Costs</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-127">陸揚原価で航海を作成する場合、原価は自動的に追加されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-127">When you create a voyage in Landed cost, costs can automatically be added to it.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-128">これらの原価は陸揚原価で設定され、さまざまな原価オプションおよび原価ベースを利用できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-128">These costs are set up in Landed cost, and various cost options and cost bases are available for them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-129">各原価は、航海の異なるレベルに対して設定され、数量、容積、重量、金額、および定義された容積除数をサポートする配分ルールを介して品目レベルに配分できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-129">Each cost can be set up for different levels of a voyage and apportioned to the item level through apportionment rules that support quantity, volume, weight, amount, and defined volumetric divisors.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-130">これらの見積原価は、航海に対するすべての原価の正確な見積を提供します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-130">These estimated costs provide an accurate estimate of all costs for a voyage.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-131">その後、陸揚原価では見積陸揚原価の暫定転記/見越計上が実行され、航海作成時に見積原価の正確な計算が提供されるようにします。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-131">Landed cost then runs a preliminary posting/accrual of the estimated landed costs to ensure that an accurate calculation of estimated costs is provided at the time of voyage creation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-132">これらの自動原価が請求されると、見積原価が取り消され、原価請求に基づいて実際原価に置き換えられます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-132">As these automatic costs are invoiced, the estimated costs are reversed and replaced by the actual costs, based on cost invoices.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-133">実際原価とは、原価のタイプ (関税、運賃、保険など) ごとに設定された精算勘定を使用して、原価請求時に転記される逆見積原価です。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-133">Actual costs are reverse estimated costs that are posted at the time of cost invoicing by using clearing accounts that are set up for each type of cost (for example, duty, freight, and insurance).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-134">これらの転記は、特定の品目に関連付けられた転記動作に従います。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-134">These postings follow the posting behavior that is associated with the specific item.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-135">転記タイプが先入れ先出し (FIFO)、後入れ先出し (LIFO)、移動加重平均、または移動平均であるかに関係なく、在庫転記は自動的に更新されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-135">They automatically update inventory posting, regardless of whether the posting type is first in, first out (FIFO), last in, first out (LIFO), moving weighted average, or moving average.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-136">すべての在庫モデル グループの転記タイプがサポートされます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-136">All inventory model group posting types are supported.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-137">移動平均および標準原価転記の場合、購買価格の差異勘定を使用して見積原価と実際原価の差額を転記できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-137">For moving average and standard cost posting, purchase price variance accounts are available to post the differences between estimated costs and actual costs.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="266c1-139">航海が元の出庫場所から最終目的地の倉庫に移動すると、ユーザーは必要に応じてその行程の各ステップ、または *区間* を更新できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-139">As a voyage moves from the originating outbound location to the final destination warehouse, users can update each step, or *leg*, of its journey as required.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-140">各区間では、リード タイムと出荷状態が識別されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-140">For each leg, a lead time and a shipment status are identified.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-141">行程の次の区間への移動に対する確定配送日も識別されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-141">Confirmed delivery dates for movement to the next leg of the journey are also identified.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-142">これらの配送日をまとめて、入庫倉庫への商品の見積配送日が表示されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-142">Together, these delivery dates provide an estimated delivery date of the goods to the inbound warehouse.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-143">ユーザーはこれらの配送日を変更できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-143">Users can change these delivery dates.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-144">この場合、商品の見積配送日は、行程のリード タイムおよび区間に基づいて自動的に更新されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-144">In this case, the estimated delivery date of the goods is then automatically updated, based on the lead times and legs of the journey.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-145">航海および船舶による移動中の商品への可視性は、商品の入庫前にコンテナ単位で利用可能です。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-145">Visibility into goods in transit by voyage and vessel is available on a per-container basis before receipt of the goods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="266c1-146">日付は発注書明細行ごとに更新されるので、企業は物流および倉庫計画をより細かく制御できます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="266c1-146">Because the dates are updated on each purchase order line, businesses have more control over logistics and warehouse planning.</span></span>
