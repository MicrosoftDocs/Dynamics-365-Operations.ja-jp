10. <span data-ttu-id="509a8-131">US-104 からの請求書は、在庫所有権変更仕訳帳が処理されたときに自動生成された発注書に対して処理されます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="509a8-131">The invoice from US-104 is processed against the purchase orders which were auto-generated when the inventory ownership change journal was processed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="509a8-132">支払は、消費された在庫の仕入先 US-104 に対してなされます。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="509a8-132">Payment is made to vendor US-104 for the inventory that was consumed.</span></span>
